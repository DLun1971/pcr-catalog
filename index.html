IP67

Ingress Protection rating — dust resistant and splash proof from any direction.

European and international certification for equipment used in explosive atmospheres. Zone 1 and Zone 2 rated.

DMR
Digital Mobile Radio standard. MOTOTRBO supports DMR Tier II conventional and Tier III trunking protocols.
